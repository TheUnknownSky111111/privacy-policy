Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

BigFoot Page-IG uses the Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Graph API to publish content to Instagram on behalf of authorized business assets. We only access the minimum data required to perform automated publishing.

Data We Access

  • Linked Facebook Page & Instagram Business Account identifiers (e.g., Page ID, IG User ID).
  • Publishing permissions to create and publish media via Graph API.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.
  • We do not collect passwords.
  • We do not store user content beyond what is required for posting.

Security

Access tokens are handled securely via server-side configuration and are not committed to source control.
