Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

The Unknown Sky operates digital media and automation tools that may interact with social media platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, or YouTube to publish videos or related content. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Data We Access

  • Page or account identifiers required for authorized publishing (e.g., Page ID, Instagram User ID, or YouTube Channel ID).
  • Permissions necessary for uploading and managing media through official APIs (e.g., Meta Graph API or YouTube Data API).

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share any personal information with third parties.
  • We do not collect or store user passwords.
  • We do not retain any user content longer than needed for automated posting or system functionality.

Security

Access tokens and authorization data are securely managed on our servers and never included in public code repositories. All sensitive data is handled with appropriate technical and organizational safeguards.

Your Rights

If you believe your information has been used inappropriately or wish to request deletion of your data, you may contact us directly for review or removal.
